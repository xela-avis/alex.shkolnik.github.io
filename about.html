Alex Shkolnik @ 2022

Alexander Shkolnik is an Assistant Professor at the Department of Statistics and Applied Probability at the University of California, Santa Barbara. He is also a Research Fellow at the Consortium for Data Analytics in Risk at the University of California, Berkeley. He obtained his PhD in computational mathematics and engineering from Stanford University in 2015. His research interests include Monte Carlo simulation of stochastic systems, high-dimensional statistics, mathematical finance, and quantitative financial risk management.
